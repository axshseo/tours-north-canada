Niagara Falls Boat Tour

Get up close and personal with the majestic Niagara Falls on our thrilling boat tour. Feel the mist on your face and the power of nature as you sail right to the base of the falls. Our experienced guides provide fascinating commentary about the history and geology of this natural wonder.

Tour Highlights

  • Up-close views of American Falls and Bridal Veil Falls
  • Expert guide commentary on the falls' history and geology
  • Comfortable boat with indoor and outdoor seating
  • Photo opportunities from the best vantage points
  • Safety equipment and experienced crew
