Banff National Park

Banff National Park is a stunning wilderness area in the Canadian Rockies, known for its turquoise lakes, majestic mountains, and abundant wildlife. It's a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Highlights

  • Lake Louise - Famous for its emerald waters and surrounding peaks
  • Moraine Lake - A picturesque lake with stunning mountain views
  • Hiking trails for all skill levels
  • Wildlife viewing opportunities
  • Hot springs and thermal baths

Top 5 Things to Do in Banff

1. Visit Lake Louise

Marvel at the emerald waters and stunning mountain backdrop of this iconic lake.

2. Hike Johnston Canyon

Explore waterfalls and dramatic canyon views on this popular hiking trail.

3. Take the Banff Gondola

Ride to the top of Sulphur Mountain for panoramic views of the Rockies.

4. Spot Wildlife

Look for elk, bighorn sheep, and bears in their natural habitat.

5. Relax in Hot Springs

Soak in the natural thermal waters at Cave and Basin National Historic Site.

Best Time to Visit Banff

Summer (June - August)

Warm weather, long days, and all facilities open. Perfect for hiking and outdoor activities.

  • Temperatures: 15-25°C (59-77°F)
  • Wildlife viewing at its peak
  • All trails and roads accessible

Winter (December - March)

Snow-covered landscapes, skiing, and fewer crowds. Magical winter wonderland.

  • Temperatures: -10 to 0°C (14-32°F)
  • Cross-country skiing and snowshoeing
  • Northern Lights viewing opportunities
