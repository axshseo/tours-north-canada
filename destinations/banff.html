Banff National Park

Banff National Park is a stunning wilderness area in the Canadian Rockies, known for its turquoise lakes, majestic mountains, and abundant wildlife. It's a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Highlights

  • Lake Louise - Famous for its emerald waters and surrounding peaks
  • Moraine Lake - A picturesque lake with stunning mountain views
  • Hiking trails for all skill levels
  • Wildlife viewing opportunities
  • Hot springs and thermal baths
