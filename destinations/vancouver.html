Vancouver

Where Mountains Meet the Sea

Top 5 Things to Do in Vancouver

1. Explore Stanley Park

Walk or bike through North America's largest urban park with seawall paths and totem poles.

2. Visit Granville Island

Discover artisan markets, theaters, and waterfront dining in this creative hub.

3. Hike Grouse Mountain

Take the Skyride gondola for panoramic views and grizzly bear habitat tours.

4. Capilano Suspension Bridge

Cross the thrilling 450-foot suspension bridge over a deep canyon.

5. Gastown Historic District

Explore cobblestone streets, steam clock, and Victorian architecture.

Stanley Park & Granville Island

Stanley Park

Vancouver's crown jewel spans 1,000 acres of lush forests, beaches, and seawall paths. Rent a bike or kayak to explore this urban oasis.

  • 27 km of seawall for walking/jogging
  • Stanley Park Train for family-friendly rides
  • Totem poles and First Nations art
  • Beaches for swimming and picnics
Plan Your Visit

Granville Island

A vibrant public market and arts district with fresh local produce, artisanal crafts, theaters, and waterfront dining.

  • Public market with local vendors
  • Emily Carr University galleries
  • Theater performances and workshops
  • Waterfront dining and craft beer

Best Time to Visit Vancouver

Summer (June - August)

Mild weather perfect for outdoor activities and festivals in Vancouver.

  • Temperatures: 18-25°C (64-77°F)
  • Stanley Park seawall and beaches
  • Outdoor concerts and markets

Winter (December - February)

Mild coastal climate with opportunities for skiing nearby and indoor attractions.

  • Temperatures: 5-10°C (41-50°F)
  • Whistler ski trips just 2 hours away
  • Holiday markets and indoor museums
