Toronto

Toronto, Canada's largest city, offers a vibrant mix of urban energy, cultural diversity, and natural beauty. From iconic landmarks to diverse neighborhoods, there's something for every traveler.

Highlights

  • CN Tower - Iconic observation tower with stunning city views
  • Toronto Islands - Relaxed escape with beaches and bike paths
  • Royal Ontario Museum - World-class art and natural history collections
  • Distillery District - Historic area with shops, galleries, and dining
  • Diverse food scene from global cuisines to local specialties
