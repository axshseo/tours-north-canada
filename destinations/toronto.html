Toronto

Canada's Vibrant Urban Heart

Top 5 Things to Do in Toronto

1. Visit the CN Tower

Ascend to the top of Canada's iconic landmark for panoramic city views.

2. Explore Distillery District

Wander through historic cobblestone streets filled with galleries, shops, and restaurants.

3. Stroll High Park

Relax in Toronto's largest public park with gardens, trails, and a zoo.

4. Visit the Royal Ontario Museum

Discover world-class exhibits of art, culture, and natural history.

5. Experience Toronto Islands

Take a ferry to these car-free islands for beaches, bike paths, and city skyline views.

Best Time to Visit Toronto

Summer (June - August)

Warm weather, festivals, and outdoor activities abound in Canada's largest city.

  • Temperatures: 20-30°C (68-86°F)
  • Toronto International Film Festival
  • Outdoor patios and beach activities

Winter (December - February)

Experience Toronto's winter charm with holiday markets and indoor attractions.

  • Temperatures: -5 to 5°C (23-41°F)
  • Christmas markets and winter festivals
  • Indoor museums and shopping
