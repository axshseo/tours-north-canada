Canada Travel Visa Guide

Official resource for understanding Canadian visa requirements

Frequently Asked Questions

Most visitors to Canada require either a visa or an Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA). Citizens of certain countries may be exempt. Check the official Government of Canada website for your specific nationality.

An eTA is an electronic travel authorization for visa-exempt foreign nationals. It's simpler and faster than a traditional visa. A visa is required for longer stays or for citizens of countries that need one. Both allow entry to Canada for tourism, business, or transit.

eTA applications are usually processed within minutes to 72 hours. Visa applications can take from a few days to several weeks, depending on your nationality and the type of visa. Apply well in advance of your travel plans.
